Nigeria

Port Harcourt is a major city in the southern part of Nigeria and the capital of Rivers State. It is located in the Niger Delta region and is surrounded by other nearby states such as Bayelsa, Delta, Imo, Abia, and Akwa Ibom. The city serves as one of the country’s most important centers for the oil and gas industry, as well as a key hub for commerce, shipping, and transportation. Port Harcourt is also known for its growing educational institutions and cultural diversity, making it an important economic and social hub in Nigeria.
